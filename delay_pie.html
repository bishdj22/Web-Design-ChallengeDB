U.S. Airport Delays as a % of Total



The pie chart above summarizes the Total Delay Percentage by U.S. airport as a percentage of the total (major U.S. airports analyzed). While Chicago O'Hare holds the largest share of delays of U.S. Airports, this does NOT make it the most delay-prone airport as it ranks as the #2 airport in the country by flight volume (only behind Atlanta). The percentage of flights delayed at O'Hare is actually just slightly above the mean and well below the most delay-prone airports (please refer to the U.S. Airport Summary graph). (September 2018-September 2019)
