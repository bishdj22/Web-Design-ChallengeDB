Project Overview

Our project looked to analyze commercial airline flights in the US in order to summarize timeliness of arrivals, and if delayed, the primary reason(s) for this (i.e. weather, carrier, mechanical issues, etc.) including how this varies by both, airline and airport.

The analysis covered 18 of the top Airlines in the U.S. and 25 of the top U.S. Airports (by total flight volume) covering the period from September 2018-September 2019.
