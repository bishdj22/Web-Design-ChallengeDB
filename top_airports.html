U.S. Airport Summary of Delays

The bar chart above summarizes the top 25 U.S. airports by flight count (left) and plots the airport delay percentage (right) versus the mean (dotted line). We found it interesting there is not necessarily a correlation between total flight count and delay percentage. As you'll notice that while Atlanta is the busiest airport, it actually ranks below the average airport delayed flight percentage; NY Metro area airports rank amongst the highest percentage of delays.
